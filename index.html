What is the bond equivalent yield calculator?
The bond equivalent yield calculator is a tool that calculates the bond investment's annual yield.
How to calculate bond equivalent yield?
To calculate bond equivalent yield you should use the following formula:
bond equivalent yield = (((face value - bond price) / bond price) * (365 / number of days to maturity)) * 100
Why use bond equivalent yield calculator?
The bond equivalent yield calculator is used in order to determine the bond investment's annual yield.
